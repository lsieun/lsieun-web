RC4 is a stream cipher and is blazingly fast compared to the available block ciphers in OpenSSL. It's certainly the fastest algorithm currently implemented in OpenSSL. RC4 is also well-regarded as an algorithm. For this reason, and due to its widespread use in SSL, it's vastly popular, though it is widely used with insecure 40-bit keys. 笔记：RC4是OpenSSL中最快的算法。

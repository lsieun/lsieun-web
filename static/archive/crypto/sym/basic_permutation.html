Definition

In cryptography, a permutation is a method of encryption by which the positions held by units of plaintext (which are commonly characters or groups of characters) are shifted according to a regular system笔记：这里regular system理解为“规则体系”比较合适, so that the ciphertext笔记：ciphertext是加密之后的数据 constitutes a permutation of the plaintext笔记：plaintext是加密前的数据。. That is, the order of the units is changed (the plaintext is reordered).

permutation的英文解释是“the reordering or rearranging of items in a group”，本质上就是打乱一组事物的原有顺序，或者说重新排列这组事物之间的顺序。这里做一个形象的比喻：在社会上，每个人都有自己的财富和社会地位，但是，经过一个大的社会动乱之后，有人从富裕变为贫穷，有人从平民变为高官，每个人的财富和社会地位都多多少少的受到影响，社会中所有人的社会地位整体发生变化的情况就可以理解为“permutation”或者说“大洗牌”。

DES Permutation

Finally, you need a permute routine. The permute routine is responsible for putting, for instance, the 57th bit of the input into the 14th bit of the output, depending on the entries in a permute_table array. As you’ll see in the DES code, this function is the workhorse埋头苦干的人；老黄牛（指吃苦耐劳的人）；耐用的机器 a person or machine that you can rely on to do hard and/or boring work of the DES algorithm; it is called dozens of times, with different permute_table each time.

Why Elliptic-Curve Cryptography?

As you can see, ECC is quite a bit more complex than “modulus” cryptography such as RSA or classic Diffie-Hellman. So, why bother with it? Speed. 笔记：既然有了RSA这样的算法，为什么要研究ECC这类算法呢？原因是使用ECC算法的计算速度快。

ECC can provide the same security with an 80-bit private key as RSA can provide with a 512-bit private key because every single bit (provably) contributes to the security of the cryptosystem. 笔记：从安全性的角度来说，ECC的80-bit private key和RSA的512-bit private key提供的安全性是一样的。

Remember that the public-key operations are O(n), where n is the number of bits in the private key. ECC is fast enough, and has a small enough operating footprint占用空间 the amount of space that sth fills, for example the amount of space that a computer takes up on a desk, that it can be used in smartcard implementations. 笔记：ECC运行快，占用内存空间小，在微型的设备上也能运行。而RSA的计算，特别是private key比较大的情况下，微型设备运算起来就很艰难，或者花费时间很长。对微型设备来说，产生大量热量、花费时间很长的功能，就很不适用。

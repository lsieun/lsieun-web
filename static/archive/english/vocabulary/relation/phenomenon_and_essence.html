Phenomenon And Essence

表面

  • appearance: 外貌；外观；外表the way that sb/sth looks on the outside; what sb/sth seems to be
  • embodiment: （体现一种思想或品质的）典型，化身 a person or thing that represents or is a typical example of an idea or a quality
  • expression: 表现 a group of signs and numbers that show a particular quantity or idea
  • form: 形式；外表；样子 the particular way sth is, seems, looks or is presented
  • reflection: 反映；显示；表达 a sign that shows the state or nature of sth
  • representation: 表现；描述；描绘；表现形式 the act of presenting sb/sth in a particular way; something that shows or describes sth

本质

  • essence: 本质；实质；精髓 the most important quality or feature of sth, that makes it what it is
  • substance: 实质；本质 the unchanging essence of something

句子

  • We should see through the appearance to the essence. 我们要透过现象看本质。

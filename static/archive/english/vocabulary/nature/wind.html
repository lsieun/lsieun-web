Hurricanes, cyclones and typhoons are all types of tropical storms. But what's the difference between them?

Well, they are all basically the same thing, but are given different names depending on where they appear.

Hurricanes are tropical storms that form over the North Atlantic Ocean and Northeast Pacific. 北美洲的东边和西边。

Cyclones are formed over the South Pacific and Indian Ocean. 太平洋的南半球和印度洋，地区包括澳大利亚的东边和西边。

Typhoons are formed over the Northwest Pacific Ocean. 位于太平洋在北半球的西边，地区包括中国东边沿海、台湾、日本、菲律宾等地区。

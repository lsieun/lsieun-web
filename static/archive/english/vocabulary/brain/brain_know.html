Know

  • common sense: Good sense and sound judgement in practical matters. 例句：it is all a matter of common sense.
  • good sense: The ability to make sound judgements and sensible decisions, especially about practical matters arising in everyday life. 例句：had the good sense to save his money。

Know

  • intuit: [动词]凭直觉知道 to know that sth is true based on your feelings rather than on facts, what sb tells you, etc.
    • intuition: [名词]直觉力the ability to know sth by using your feelings rather than considering the facts

词组

  • the true meaning of: 别人对某个概念有误解的时候，可以用这个词组。
  • scratch the surface of: 对某种事情知之甚少；浅尝即止；只触及表面；只作肤浅的探讨
    • 例句： In this short talk, I can only scratch the surface of the database and its implications. 在这篇短文中，我只能截取数据库的一点皮毛和启示。
    • 例句： Once cooled, scratch the surface of the potatoes with a fork. 一旦冷却，用一把叉子戳马铃薯的表面。
